To add mods to spiderheck, download the either of the following mods as well as Modweaver (allows the mods to actually work)
Then watch the epic video that should be at the bottom of the page. That'll show you how to extract modweaver properly as well as how to load the mods
Basically, you need to put the mod dll files in SpiderHeck/modweaver/mods folder.
The mods do not get their own separate folders, all the dlls just chill out with each other in that one location.
Make sure to launch spiderheck game after extracting modweaver so it can create all the necessary folders and whatever, then you can close out of spiderheck and add the mods
Also, if you close the terminal/command window that opens up with spiderheck, it will close SH as well, so just minimize that cmd window and carry on
